How to take part

Four ways in. Pick yours.

Use /cupsignup in Telegram to claim one of the 48 spots and get your nation. Sign up, get drawn, represent.

Not playing? Use /support to back a nation and rep its flag. Ride with your country all the way to the final.

Predict

Match format

Spin the wheel. Highest score wins.

Group stage

72 matches, 12 groups

The wheel picks the game. Both players get 3 lives. Highest score takes the match. Top of each group goes through.

Knockouts

Win or go home

Same rules, higher stakes. 5 lives each, highest score wins, loser is out. No second chances from here.

Semi-finals

Best of 5

Every round spins a fresh game. 3 lives each, highest score takes the round. First to 3 rounds makes the final.

The final

Best of 7

Same as the semis, stretched to the limit. First to 4 rounds is champion of the PLAY EVERYWHERE CUP.

Zig Zag Endless Maze Colour Run Spinning Around Circle Leap Arrow Racer Spikes Everywhere Colour Matcher

Played across 8 PlaysOut games. Prizes paid across the top 8, with the champion taking the biggest share.
