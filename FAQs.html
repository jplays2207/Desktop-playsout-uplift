FAQs

How Do I Get More Lives?

Some games on PlaysOut have no life limits, allowing unlimited play. Others may have limits, but lives replenish quickly. When you run out, you can watch ads to earn extra lives instantly.

Can I Play Offline?

PlaysOut is currently an online gaming platform. For the best experience, please ensure you’re connected to the internet while playing.

Can I Buy and Use Gems and Gold for All Games?

Yes.

Gems currently let you skip ads during gameplay. Soon, we’ll launch games where Gems can buy in-game items.

Gold can be earned for free by watching ads in the Store, then exchanged for Gems. The more Gems you own, the greater your contribution to our platform—which may lead to higher rewards in future airdrop events!

What Is a Gift Package?

Gift Packages are special rewards created by PlaysOut, containing benefits like:

Ad-free gameplay

Gold coins

Exclusive in-game items

Currently, not all users receive these packages. We’re developing tailored packages for different regions and user types soon!

Why Do I Have Gift Packages for Only Some Games?

Not all games support Gift Packages, but over 30 games already do.

Packages are excluded from certain games to preserve gameplay balance and fairness.

Where Do I See My Game Credits?

Game credits aren’t visible in the current version, but our system tracks all player credits.

Future updates will add features like leaderboards, with rewards for high-scoring players!
